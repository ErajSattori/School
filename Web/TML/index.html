The Toronto Maple Leafs are a professional ice hockey team based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. They are a member of the Atlantic Division in the Eastern Conference of the National Hockey League. The team is one of the original six teams in the league. They are represented by chairman Larry Tanenbaum and managed by GM Dave Norris. The head coach is Peter Horachek. The leafs have played at the Air Canada Centre since 1999. The Leafs are the most valuable franchise in the NHL with an estimated worth of $1.15 billion as of 2014.

The classic Maple Leafs jersey is one of the best-selling jerseys of the NHL worn among fans. The current logo is a maple leaf as seen above, but they used to have a letter "T" that appeared on a blue shield.
