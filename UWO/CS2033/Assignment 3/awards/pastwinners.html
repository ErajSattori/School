Awards: Past

The Toronto Maple Leafs have had many players that have won individual awards including the Hart Memorial Trophy, the Bill Masterton Trophy, the James Norris Trophy, the Frank J. Selke Trophy, and the Vezina Trophy.

Individual Winners in the history of the Leafs:

  • Hart Memorial Trophy
    • Babe Pratt (1944)
    • Ted Kennedy (1955)
    • Pat Burns (1993)

  • Bill Masterton Trophy
    • Jason Blake (2008)
    • Gaye Stewart (1943)
    • Syl Apps (1937)

  • James Norris Trophy
    • No winners to show yet.

  • Frank J. Selke Trophy
    • Doug Gilmour (1993)
    • Dave Keon (1967)

  • Vezina Trophy
    • Terry Swchuk (1965)
    • Johnny Bower (1961)
    • Harry Lumley (1954)
    • Al Rollins (1951)
    • Turk Broda (1946-49)
