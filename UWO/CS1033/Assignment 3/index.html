Micro Building Designs is a family owned and operated business selling and installing Perfect Sheds by Cabanons Fontaine. Perfect Sheds are available in many styles and sizes or can be custom built to suit your needs. Whether you need a pool house, workshop, garage, home office, cottage bunkie or kid's playhouse, your imagination is the limit. We even do garden and tool sheds! We offer concrete work and landscaping as well if you would like your shed installed on a pad, or need a new patio, walkway or stone barrier with some plants around your shed.
