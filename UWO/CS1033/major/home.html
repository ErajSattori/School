Overview

Ben Haggerty, mainly known by his stage name Macklemore, is an American rapper that collaborates with his producer Ryan Lewis to produce music albums. The most popular song they produced "Thrift Shop", reached number 1 on the U.S. Billboard Top 100 in 2013. Macklemore has recieved seven Grammy nominations throughout his career and is still performing live on tour.

  • explore Macklemore's fame on social media platforms
